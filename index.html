About Us

Who We Are

Gravity Metal Private Limited is a leading aluminium wire rod manufacturer based in Raipur, Chhattisgarh. We are committed to delivering consistent quality and reliable supply to conductor manufacturers, cable industries, and rolling mills across India.

Our state-of-the-art facility near Raipur uses advanced casting and rolling technology to produce EC Grade, Commercial Grade, and Flipping Coil aluminium wire rods to meet the most demanding industry specifications.

Advanced Manufacturing Facility

Modern aluminium casting and continuous rolling setup with world-class machinery — engineered for dimensional precision, consistent surface quality, and high output reliability.

In-House Quality Lab with World-Class Equipment

We operate a dedicated quality lab equipped with world-class testing machinery. Every lot undergoes rigorous mechanical and electrical testing — tensile strength, elongation and conductivity — before dispatch. A test certificate accompanies every single order.

Our Process

01
Raw Material
Premium grade aluminium ingots sourced from certified suppliers
02
Melting & Alloying
Precision melting in induction furnaces with strict composition control
03
Continuous Casting
CCR technology for uniform grain structure and consistent surface quality
04
Rolling
Multi-pass rolling mill producing precise, consistent wire rod dimensions
05
Quality Control
Dedicated in-house lab with world-class testing machinery — tensile strength, elongation and electrical conductivity verified on every single lot before approval
06
Coiling & Dispatch
Approved lots are neatly coiled, strapped and dispatched with a test certificate — ensuring traceability from plant to customer
